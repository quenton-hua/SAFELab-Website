Work With Us

The SAFE Lab is always looking for talented and motivated undergraduate students and graduate students with an aptitude for collaborative work. Use the “Contact Us” tab to apply for a position and fill out the form according to the guidelines below. If you would like to talk to Dr. Currie-Gregg directly, contact her at currie-gregg@tamu.edu.

Graduate Students

If you are currently a Texas A&M University graduate student looking for a lab, please indicate if you are a Masters or Ph. D. student at the beginning of your message. Include your transcript, a short description of what you have worked on (please be sure to include any relevant skills), what projects you would like to work on, and what your career goals are. If you are looking for a graduate position for next year and you are not currently a student at Texas A&M, you will need to apply to the university graduate aerospace or industrial engineering program.

Undergraduate Students

If you are an undergraduate student at Texas A&M, please include your transcript and a short paragraph indicating your motivation and prior experience (please be sure to include any relevant skills).
